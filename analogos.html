Perfect Imperfection

2025

Glitches and analog distortions are inherently unpredictable, emerging spontaneously from technological failures or deviations. Unlike structured digital errors, these artifacts do not follow a fixed path or linear hierarchy but instead grow in a decentralized and interconnected manner. This non-hierarchical and evolving nature aligns with the concept of the 'rhizomatic' as proposed by Deleuze and Guattari (1987), where structures form through multiplicities rather than singular origins or fixed sequences Análogos emerges from a deep engagement with the aesthetics of analog technology and its intrinsic imperfections. The project reflects on the transient nature of media, where degradation, distortion, and noise become expressive tools rather than flaws.
