Análogos

2025

Glitches and analog distortions are inherently unpredictable, emerging spontaneously from technological failures or deviations. Unlike structured digital errors, these artifacts do not follow a fixed path or linear hierarchy but instead grow in a decentralized and interconnected manner. Análogos reflects on the transient nature of media, where degradation, distortion, and noise become expressive tools rather than flaws.

Instalation at Centro de Memória Vila do Conde (2025).
