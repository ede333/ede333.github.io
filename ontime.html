OnTime

2024

ONTIME integrates interactive elements, with sound produced in Ableton Live and Plug Data, a custom synthesizer using DaisySeed, lighting managed through MAX SP, QLC+, Leapmotion, and visuals created with Touchdesigner and Madmapper. The performance also features choreographed interactions, adding a theatrical aspect. All system components where custom built, enabling precise control over both physical and digital materials. The performance is modular and dinamic allowing to move it and perform it in diferent configurations and diferent places depending on the condicions and space that we have.
