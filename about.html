About Me

Ede, the artistic pseudonym of Diogo Nóbrega, is an experimental electronic musician, multi-instrumentalist, and multimedia artist. Born in Madeira Island, he discovered his passion for music at an early age. He was fascinated by learning different instruments, nurturing a continuous desire to explore the world of sound.

Today, he defines himself as a multimedia artist with a particular focus on sound. Driven by experimentalism, he finds inspiration in creating new sounds, often using unconventional or non-typically musical elements. He always seeks to integrate his minimalist sounds with visuals that convey the same feeling and aims to create innovative music and experiences through unique combinations of different styles and sounds.
