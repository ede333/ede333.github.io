About Me

My name is Diogo Nóbrega. As a Multimedia artist, my best skill sets lies in 3D software, live performances, and sound design. I am particularly passionate about music and VJing, merging experimental sound with electronic music and generative visuals.

In addition to my creative pursuits, Im exploring into the realms of creative and visual programming, as well as interactive installations using software like MadMapper, MAX MPS and TouchDesigner.

I also like physical computing.
