Outer Gravity

OUTER GRAVITY is a 3D animated short trailer. The main goal was to explore the world of procedural texturing in Blender. That way, almost every texture is procedural. From the planets, the desert, the ice/snow, the engine flames and even the black hole are entirely procedural.
