Meteorito

2025

After finding a mysterious online advertisement for a supposed meteorite with magical properties, we decided to purchase it. The seller informed us that the meteorite could only be activated through sound. Meteorite is our attempt at the activation ritual.

The installation takes place in a cold, smoke-filled basement with minimal lighting. Visitors experience the meteorite's sonic awakening through extreme noise and distortions at dangerous volume levels. Ear protection is mandatory and visits are limited to 10 minutes maximum.

Enter at your own risk.
